Hi!

I'm a telecom-turned-biomedical-turned-software engineer.

Message me: mail [at] guillego.com

Born and raised in Madrid, Spain, moved to Copenhagen a few years ago, where my favorite things are cycling through the city, jumping into the sea, eating pastries and the long days of summer.

Professionally I'm on the look for the next thing that captures my imagination. I'm particularly attracted to distributed systems and have found a lot of joy when working with Elixir and most recently in Go. I'm also genuinely curious about the electric grid and how we could bring distributed systems concepts to how to manage it in a less centralized way. In the past couple of years I spent some time building a distributed IoT sports camera system and developing a computer vision virtual coach for racket sports at Clutch.
Previously spent a few years in the health-tech domain, augmenting radiology at Radiobotics.

I like to capture life with my camera, take trips on my bike, rock climb and write. I devour podcasts, books, wikipedia, blogs and any source of interesting information. Is there such thing as being too curious?.
